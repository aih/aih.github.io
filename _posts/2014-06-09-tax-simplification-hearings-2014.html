--- layout: post title: Tax Simplification Hearings - 2014 date: '2014-06-09T09:52:00.002-07:00' author: Ari Hershowitz tags: - tax law - 21stcenturytaxation - Orrin Hatch - Ron Wyden - Senate Finance Committee - Tax Code - tax modified_time: '2014-06-09T09:53:26.424-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-7029518648320057552.post-2856194885063963968 blogger_orig_url: https://blog.linkedlegislation.com/2014/06/tax-simplification-hearings-2014.html --- Annette Nellen at 21st Century Tax has written a post on Congressional hearings this summer on tax reform. The Senate Finance Committee, led by Senators Wyden (D-OR) and Hatch (R-UT), will consider topics including:


  • Education incentives
  • Identity theft and privacy
  • "Modernizing" corporate taxation
  • Tax simplification
As I mentioned in a comment on Annette's blog, I would like to see Silicon Valley weigh in, particularly on tax simplification, with ideas to simplify the code and technology to simplify tax filing. I have discussed this before, including the great potential to use (big) data from hundreds of millions of actual filings to simplify tax law and the practice of filing.
